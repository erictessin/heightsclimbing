Membership

Membership Info

Interested in becoming a member of Heights? Becoming a member will not only enrich you physically and mentally, but will give you a home away from home. Come here to socialize, relax, lounge, and of course... climb! A membership also provides you with additional benefits including discounts to our store, free fitness classes, and special member-only events. Whether this is your first time climbing or your a returning crusher, Heights has what you are looking for!
